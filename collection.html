Featured Masterpiece

Luna Masterpiece

The Luna Masterpiece represents the pinnacle of horological artistry. This exceptional timepiece features a hand-assembled Swiss movement with a mesmerizing moonphase complication that accurately tracks lunar cycles for 122 years.

Crafted from 950 platinum with a hand-guilloché dial, each Luna Masterpiece takes over 300 hours to complete. The exhibition caseback reveals the intricate movement finished with Côtes de Genève striping and blued screws.
