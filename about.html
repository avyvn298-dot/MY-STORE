OUR STORY

160 Years of Swiss Watchmaking Excellence

SINCE 1860

A LEGACY OF PRECISION

Founded in the heart of the Swiss Jura mountains, ACUMEN has been synonymous with precision, innovation, and timeless elegance for over a century and a half.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create timepieces that transcend mere functionality, becoming true works of art that tell stories across generations.

Today, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing innovation, crafting watches that meet the demands of modern life without compromising on traditional Swiss excellence.

OUR MILESTONES

1860

Foundation

Edouard Acumen establishes the first workshop in La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland.

1916

Innovation Era

Introduction of the first stopwatch with 1/100th second precision.

1963

Carrera Launch

The iconic Carrera chronograph debuts, inspired by motorsport legends.

1969

Monaco Revolution

World's first automatic chronograph with revolutionary square case design.

2004

Aquaracer Series

Professional diving watches combining luxury with extreme functionality.

2025

Future Forward

Continuing innovation with sustainable materials and cutting-edge technology.

SWISS CRAFTSMANSHIP
